Welcome to the Church of God Community

WHAT WE DO

Welcome to our Church

Sunday Worship Gatherings (Weekly)

Every Sunday morning, our congregation comes together for a time of worship, reflection, and community. We sing hymns, listen to inspiring sermons, and share in communion. It’s a sacred space where hearts connect and faith is strengthened.

Community Outreach (Monthly)

Once a month, we organize community outreach events. Whether it’s distributing food to those in need, visiting nursing homes, or cleaning up local parks, we believe in serving our neighbors. These acts of kindness extend the love of Christ beyond our church walls.

Prayer Nights (Biweekly)

Every other Wednesday evening, we gather for focused prayer. We intercede for our church family, our city, and global needs. It’s a time to seek God’s guidance, lift up burdens, and experience the power of collective prayer.
